    6.52k/48b/6.25k

            % -----------------------------------
    6.52k/48b/6.25k

            % In order to minimize numerical round off for points that are
    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

            % numbers.  Rather than convert each point to ECEF, then
    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

            %
    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k

    6.52k/48b/6.25k
